Explore the beauty of Rome through these stunning photos of its heritage sites.

The Colosseum, an ancient amphitheater in Rome, is a symbol of the Roman Empire.

Vatican City, the smallest independent state in the world, is the spiritual center of the Catholic Church.

The Sistine Chapel, a Vatican City chapel, is famous for its Michelangelo-painted ceiling.
